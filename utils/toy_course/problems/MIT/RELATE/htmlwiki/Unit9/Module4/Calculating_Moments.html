Calculating Moments of Inertia

Learning goals

After working through this module, you should be able to:

  • Apply the concept of moment of inertia to different objects.
  • Choose the correct form of moment of inertia based on choice of axis

Common Moments of Inertia

The tables below give the moment of inertia for commonly encountered rigid bodies of total mass M and uniform density rotating about a specific axis that passes through the body's center of mass.

Note that the moment of inertia depends on the chosen axis. The same object can have different moments of inertia correpsonding to rotation around different axes.

Description Viewed from Beside the Axis Viewed Along the Axis Moment of Inertia

Thin Disk about Axis
Through the Edge 		\(\frac{1}{4}MR^{2}\)
