Applying Newton's Laws

In this unit we will apply Newton's Laws to physical situations that are a little bit more complex than the examples we have seen in the previous unit. We introduce two key concepts of the SIM method: the System and the idea that it creates the distinction between forces that are internal versus external.

This unit is focused on the Linear Dynamics and Net Force core model.

Learning Goals

At the end of this unit you should be able to:

  • Define the term "system" as it is used in mechanics.
  • Given a multi-object system, distinguish internal forces from external forces.
  • Draw free body diagrams for situations involving several moving objects.
  • Apply Newton's Second Law to situations involving several moving objects constrained to move with different accelerations (e.g. due to a pulley and strings).
