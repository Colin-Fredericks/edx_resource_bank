Introductory Quiz

It may seem strange to start a course with a quiz, but it is important for you to take this quiz, especially because this course is for people with "some knowledge of mechanics":

  1. It will give us important insight into what you and the class bring to the course in terms of various skills.
  2. It will give you practice taking a quiz (and will not count in your grade).
  3. It will give you an idea of the variety of problem types you'll experience in this course.
  4. In the future analysis of these data we will be able to give new students guidance on whether they're ready for this course.

So we hope you will make a conscientious effort to do well on this quiz.
