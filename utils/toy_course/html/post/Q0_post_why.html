Post-Course Quiz

As we started with a quiz, we also end with one. If you took Quiz Zero at the beginning of the course, we recommend you take this one as well.

  1. It will give us important insight into what you learned during the course
  2. It will not count in your grade.

So we hope you will make a conscientious effort to do well on this quiz.
